The p tag is for paragraphs.

Paragraphs can go on for many lines. It is good to know that a new line is inserted after a paragraph too. We will talk more about this later but paragraphs and headings are called "block" elements. A new line is inserted after a "block" element. "In-line" elements don't have a new line inserted after them.
